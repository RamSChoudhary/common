Password Generator


A strong password is characterized by complexity, including a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. It should be sufficiently long, typically exceeding 12 characters, to enhance resistance against brute-force attacks. Unpredictability is crucial, avoiding easily guessable information like birthdays or common words. Regular updates and unique passwords for different accounts further fortify digital security.
